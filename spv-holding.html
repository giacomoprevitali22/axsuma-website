UK SPV and Holding Companies

End-to-end support for the creation and administration of UK Special Purpose Vehicles (SPVs) and UK holding companies.

Whether you are structuring an investment, executing a transaction, acquiring UK assets, or establishing a long-term corporate presence, we deliver compliant, efficient, and professionally managed corporate structures tailored to your objectives.

Our services ensure that your UK entity is properly formed, fully compliant with local statutory obligations, and supported by experienced Axsuma directors who understand the demands of cross-border investments and regulated transactions.

UK SPVs

UK SPVs are widely used for asset ownership, financing structures, securitisations, joint ventures, and risk-isolation strategies. We establish SPVs quickly and in line with UK regulatory and commercial requirements.

We specialise in SPVs for:

  • Property acquisitions and developments
  • Private equity transactions
  • Fund structures and co-investment vehicles
  • Securitisation and structured finance
  • Joint ventures and corporate restructurings

UK Holding Companies

A UK holding company offers a stable legal environment, access to a comprehensive double tax treaty network, and clear corporate governance standards. We assist domestic and international clients in establishing holding companies for investment, consolidation, or strategic ownership purposes.

The use of UK companies as holding companies is an attractive proposition given the extensive double tax treaties available can significantly reduce any impact to dividend withholding tax and further strengthened with the introduction of the Substantial Shareholder Exemption rules.
