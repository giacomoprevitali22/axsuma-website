Identity Verification (ID&V)

Companies House authorised identity verification as an ACSP.

Identity verification is now a legal requirement.

From 18 November 2026, all directors, LLP members, PSCs, and individuals filing on behalf of companies must verify their identity with Companies House. Axsuma is a Companies House Authorised Corporate Service Provider (ACSP).

Start Your Verification

To begin the identity verification process, please provide details for each individual requiring verification using the form on our main website. Our team will guide you through each step.
