Register of Overseas Entities Terms

Last updated: 14 April 2026

ROE Verification and Compliance Services

These terms apply specifically to services relating to the Register of Overseas Entities (ROE) and beneficial ownership verification for overseas entities acquiring or owning UK property. They supplement our Standard Terms of Engagement.

Scope of ROE Services

Axsuma provides comprehensive Register of Overseas Entities services including:

  • Verification of beneficial ownership information in accordance with Companies House requirements
  • Preparation and submission of ROE applications to Companies House
  • Verification of overseas entity details and corporate structure
  • Obtaining verification of beneficial ownership from appropriate authorised institutions
  • Management of ongoing compliance reporting requirements
  • Updates and amendments to ROE registrations following changes in beneficial ownership
  • Advice on regulatory requirements and deadlines

Our services are provided in accordance with the Overseas Entities Regulations 2022 and Companies House guidance. We will advise you of any regulatory changes that may affect your obligations.

Your Responsibilities and Eligibility

You warrant that:

  • You are legally entitled to engage Axsuma to act on your behalf regarding ROE matters
  • All information you provide to us is accurate, complete, and truthful
  • The beneficial ownership information provided is genuine and not subject to sanctions
  • Your entity is acquiring, holding, or has previously acquired UK property as required by ROE regulations
  • You will not use our services to circumvent beneficial ownership transparency requirements or facilitate financial crime
  • You have obtained all necessary internal authorisations and approvals to instruct us

You remain responsible for the accuracy of beneficial ownership information. Companies House verifies beneficial ownership data, and any false information may expose you to significant penalties and regulatory investigation.

Verification and Documentation

ROE compliance requires verification of beneficial ownership by an authorised institution. You must:

  • Provide complete and accurate beneficial ownership information
  • Cooperate with our requests for supporting documentation (passports, corporate structure charts, certificates of incorporation, etc.)
  • Allow us to arrange verification through authorised institutions (such as banks, accountants, or legal advisers)
  • Ensure beneficial owners cooperate with verification processes
  • Provide certified or official copies of documents in English or accompanied by certified translation
  • Update information promptly if circumstances change

Incomplete or unclear documentation will delay the verification process. We will work with you to obtain necessary information, but delays arising from your failure to provide documents cannot be attributed to us.

Companies House Filing and Deadlines

We will prepare ROE applications in accordance with the Overseas Entities Regulations 2022. Key regulatory deadlines include:

  • Initial registration within a specified timeframe of acquiring UK property (typically 6 months from acquisition for property acquired before 1 January 2022; 14 days for acquisitions after that date)
  • Annual updates due before 31 January each year following initial registration
  • Updating within the required timeframe following any change to beneficial ownership
  • Filing cessation notices if the entity ceases to own UK property

It is your responsibility to ensure you are aware of applicable deadlines. We will send reminder notices, but you must ensure sufficient notice is given to allow timely processing. Failure to comply with filing deadlines may result in regulatory penalties and Companies House enforcement action.

Changes to Beneficial Ownership

Any changes to beneficial ownership must be reported to Companies House within specified timeframes (typically 14 days of change). You must:

  • Notify us immediately of any changes to beneficial ownership or ownership structure
  • Provide updated beneficial ownership information and supporting documentation
  • Ensure new beneficial owners cooperate with identity verification requirements
  • Confirm that changes are accurate and truthful before we submit updates

Updates may require new verification. We will advise of additional fees if re-verification is required due to changes in beneficial ownership. Delays in notifying us of changes may result in failure to comply with regulatory deadlines.

Fees and Additional Costs

ROE service fees are set out in the engagement letter and typically include:

  • Initial ROE registration and verification
  • Preparation and Companies House filing of application
  • Annual compliance updates
  • Initial beneficial ownership verification

Additional charges may apply for:

  • Additional beneficial owners requiring separate verification
  • Complex corporate structures requiring extensive investigation
  • Changes to beneficial ownership requiring re-verification
  • Document translation or certified copy preparation
  • Expedited verification or filing arrangements
  • Additional correspondence or communications beyond standard scope

All out-of-pocket costs (including Companies House filing fees and verification service charges) are recharged to you separately.

Data Protection and Beneficial Ownership Secrecy

Beneficial ownership information is sensitive and is treated with appropriate confidentiality protections:

  • Personal data is processed in accordance with GDPR and our Data Protection Policy
  • Access to beneficial ownership information is limited to Axsuma staff involved in delivery of services and Companies House staff administering the ROE
  • Information is transmitted securely and stored in accordance with data protection requirements
  • Once filed with Companies House, beneficial ownership information may be subject to Companies House access rules and fraud prevention checks
  • We do not disclose beneficial ownership information except to Companies House or as required by law

You should be aware that once registered with Companies House, beneficial ownership information may be accessed by law enforcement, financial intelligence units, and other authorities in accordance with Companies House policy.

Liability Limitations

Our liability for ROE services is limited to the fees paid in the preceding 12 months or GBP 100,000, whichever is lower. We are not liable for:

  • Regulatory penalties imposed by Companies House arising from false or inaccurate beneficial ownership information you provide
  • Your failure to notify us of changes to beneficial ownership
  • Your failure to meet filing deadlines if proper notice was not given
  • Consequences of your failure to follow our advice or recommendations regarding compliance
  • Delays caused by third parties (verification service providers, postal services, Companies House processing times)
  • Issues arising from complex corporate structures or opaque ownership arrangements

You remain responsible for the accuracy and completeness of beneficial ownership information submitted to Companies House. Any regulatory consequences arising from incorrect information are your responsibility.

Termination of ROE Services

Either party may terminate ROE services with 30 days' written notice. Upon termination:

  • We will complete any work in progress and deliver outstanding deliverables
  • You remain responsible for future ROE compliance and filing deadlines
  • You must appoint a replacement adviser to manage ongoing ROE obligations
  • Outstanding fees must be paid before we release any documents or information

Termination does not relieve you of ROE regulatory compliance obligations. You must ensure continuous management of your ROE registration.
