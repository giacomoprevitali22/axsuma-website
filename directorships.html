UK Professional Director Services

Independent UK Board Representation

Independent UK Board Representation

We provide professional UK resident director services to support leading law firms, private client advisers, fiduciary intermediaries supporting international families, entrepreneurs, and substantial corporate groups.

Our service is designed for structures requiring:

  • A credible UK-resident statutory director
  • Independent governance oversight
  • UK management and control positioning
  • Enhanced substance
  • Reduced nominee exposure risk

Typical Structures

We regularly support:

  • UK holding companies
  • UK SPVs holding investment or real estate assets
  • Family office structures
  • Pre-transaction or exit structuring vehicles
  • Funds
  • Private investment and wealth planning structures

Each appointment is assessed individually, with a focus on commercial rationale, governance clarity, and long-term sustainability.

Appointment as UK Statutory Director

  • Formal Companies House registration
  • Inclusion in statutory registers
  • Fulfilment of fiduciary duties under the Companies Act 2006
  • Independent oversight of board decisions

Governance & Decision Oversight

  • Participation in board meetings (as required)
  • Review of material contracts and transactions
  • Documentation of resolutions
  • Oversight of statutory filings and compliance

Management & Control Support

Where appropriate, our involvement assists with:

  • Establishing UK board substance
  • Supporting tax residency positioning (subject to client's tax advice)
  • Providing comfort to banks and counterparties
  • Strengthening governance credibility
