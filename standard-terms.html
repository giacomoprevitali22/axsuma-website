Standard Terms for Corporate Services

These Terms of Engagement set out the basis on which Axsuma Ltd provides corporate services to clients. By engaging Axsuma, you agree to be bound by these terms. If you require service-specific terms, please refer to the relevant service terms document.

Scope of Services

Axsuma provides a range of corporate services including company formations, governance support, M&A transaction management, accounting services, and specialist compliance services. The specific services to be provided will be detailed in an engagement letter or service proposal signed by both parties. Services are provided on the basis that you have obtained all necessary internal approvals and authorisations before instructing us.

Our services are limited to the specific scope agreed in writing. Any services beyond the agreed scope will be considered separately and will require additional instruction and fee agreement. We reserve the right to decline any instruction that falls outside our area of expertise or where we have conflicts of interest.

Client Responsibilities

Your cooperation is essential to our ability to deliver services effectively. You agree to:

  • Provide accurate, complete, and timely information and documents required for service delivery
  • Ensure that information provided is truthful and not misleading
  • Maintain confidentiality of our advice and communications
  • Notify us promptly of any changes to circumstances that may affect our services
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Make decisions on matters that are your responsibility and not defer these to us

We cannot be held responsible for delays or errors arising from incomplete, inaccurate, or late information provided by you or failure to meet these responsibilities.

Fees and Payment Terms

Our fees are set out in the engagement letter or proposal and are based on the scope and complexity of the services required. Fees may be calculated on the basis of:

  • Fixed fees: A predetermined fixed amount for the complete service
  • Time-based fees: Calculated at agreed hourly rates, with estimates provided where possible
  • Transactional fees: Based on the nature or value of the transaction
  • Retainer fees: For ongoing corporate services covering a defined scope

Invoices are payable within 30 days of issue unless otherwise agreed. Payment should be made to the bank details provided on the invoice. If fees are not paid within 30 days, we reserve the right to suspend services and charge interest on overdue amounts in accordance with the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998. Costs of recovery (including legal fees) will be charged to the client.

Expenses and disbursements (such as Companies House filing fees, professional adviser costs, or third-party fees) will be recharged to you in addition to our professional fees. We will keep expenses to a reasonable level and will inform you in advance of any significant costs.

Confidentiality and Professional Privilege

Axsuma treats all client information as confidential and will not disclose it to third parties except as required by law, regulation, or where we have your express consent. Information provided to us is protected by professional privilege to the extent permitted by law.

We may disclose information to regulatory bodies (such as Companies House, HMRC, or the FCA), law enforcement, or courts where required by law or legal process. We are required to comply with anti-money laundering regulations and may conduct due diligence checks, including verification of identity and beneficial ownership.

You acknowledge that we may share limited information with our insurers, auditors, and service providers solely for legitimate business purposes and subject to strict confidentiality obligations. Information relating to your company that is registered at Companies House or other public registers is not confidential.

Termination and Suspension of Services

Either party may terminate the engagement by written notice. Termination does not relieve you of the obligation to pay for services rendered prior to termination. We may terminate or suspend services immediately if:

  • Payment of fees is more than 30 days overdue
  • You breach a material term of these Terms of Engagement
