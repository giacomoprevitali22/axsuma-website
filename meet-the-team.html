Meet the Team

Our founders and senior team bring decades of experience from leading institutions combined with a relationship-first approach.

Our founders and senior team bring decades of experience from Magic Circle law firms, Big 4 accountancy practices and leading corporate service providers. They combine institutional expertise with a relationship-first approach, so you get technically robust, regulator-ready advice delivered with commercial sense and personal accountability.

Founders & Managing Directors

Jason Reader

Managing Director - Operations

Jason is the founder and Managing Director – Operations, with over 30 years' experience in the Corporate Services sector.

Jason has led UK operational entities, overseeing company formations, secretariat functions, legal process agent services, registers of overseas entities, and client acceptance and onboarding for regulated services.

He supports complex global business structures and drives client outcomes, including supporting UK directorships for both short-term transactional objectives and longer-term non-executive or fractional roles.

Charlotte Phipps-Hornby

Managing Director - Clients

Charlotte is the founder and Managing Director – Clients, with over 20 years' experience in international professional services.

Charlotte advises VC- and PE-backed growth companies, multinationals, and investment funds on cross-border expansion, governance, compliance, and complex transactions.

She combines commercial leadership with deep technical expertise to deliver scalable, high-quality solutions for international clients.

Leadership Team

Joanne Reader

Finance Manager

Joanne is a qualified accountant who has operated in client accounting roles and in-house finance management roles. Joanne has a wealth of experience in Payroll, bookkeeping and VAT gained through her previous roles, including previous corporate services experience with Jordans.

Joanne is a dedicated member of the team and always on hand to support clients in respect of their finance, payroll, bookkeeping and VAT queries.

Sarah Youll

Accounting & Tax Advisory

Sarah is a Chartered Accountant and Tax Advisor who trained as a Chartered Accountant with big 4 firm, PwC. She then went on to be a Director for an accounting firm with over 500 clients in the hospitality industry. Sarah has gained substantial corporate service experience whilst working for Vistra.

Sarah is passionate about working with business owners and their management teams providing financial and operational advice that supports their business to succeed on every level.

Martin Palmer

Consultant

Martin has worked in the UK and international trust and corporate services sector for nearly 40 years.

He has a law degree from Nottingham University, and in 2012 was awarded the Advanced Diploma in International Taxation from the UK Chartered Institute of Taxation.

He has spoken and written widely on corporate law and international tax matters since the mid 1990's. His written output includes articles for the Journal of International Tax Trust & Corporate Planning and a number of the Special Supplements of the well known legal textbook Gore Browne on Companies. He has written a book entitled 'UK Companies: Tax Advantages for International Business' now in its 5th Edition, published by Lexis Nexis.
