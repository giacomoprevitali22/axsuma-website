Cookie Policy

Axsuma Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy explains how Axsuma uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website. Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us improve your experience by remembering your preferences, analysing website usage, and ensuring core functionality.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit a website. Each time you visit, your browser sends these cookies back to the website, allowing it to recognize you and remember information about your previous visits.

Cookies serve various purposes depending on their type. Some are essential for website functionality, while others help us understand how you use our site, remember your preferences, or deliver targeted content. Cookies can be temporary (session cookies, deleted when you close your browser) or persistent (stored on your device for a set period).

How We Use Cookies

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as page navigation, access to secure areas, and form submission. These cookies are required for our website to work and cannot be disabled without affecting functionality.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies help us understand how visitors use our website. We use analytics tools to track page views, user interactions, and website performance. This information helps us improve our website's speed, content, and user experience. Data collected is anonymous and aggregated.
  • Functional Cookies: These cookies remember your choices and preferences to provide a more personalised experience. They might remember language preferences, search history, or other settings you've selected during your visit.
  • Advertising/Targeting Cookies: We do not use third-party advertising or targeting cookies to track your behaviour across the internet or deliver personalized advertisements based on your browsing history.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party service providers, such as analytics platforms, content delivery networks, or other service partners we work with. These third-party cookies are used in accordance with their own privacy policies and terms of service. We encourage you to review their privacy policies to understand how they use cookies and your personal data.

While we minimize reliance on third-party cookies, some non-intrusive analytics services may be enabled to help us improve our website. We do not share personal data with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

Your Cookie Choices

You have full control over cookies on your device. Most web browsers allow you to manage cookie settings through their preferences or settings menu. You can choose to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Block all cookies (which may affect website functionality)
  • Delete cookies already stored on your device
  • Be notified when websites try to set cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites

Please note that disabling certain cookies, particularly strictly necessary cookies, may affect the functionality of our website and your ability to access certain features. If you disable performance and analytics cookies, we won't be able to gather information about how you use our site to improve it.
